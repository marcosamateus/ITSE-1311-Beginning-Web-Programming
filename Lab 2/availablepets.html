Pet Adoption Fees

The most an adoption fee for any dog, cat, puppy, or kitten is $80. Many animals are listed as VIPs and their adoption fees are only $40! On occasion, the shelter may have special adoption fees that are even less.

The adoption fee includes spay/neuter surgery, vaccinations, heartworm test and treatment, Microchip and Collar and tags.

These services could easily cost several hundred to more than a thousand dollars at a private veterinary clinic if you were to get a “free” pet from elsewhere.
