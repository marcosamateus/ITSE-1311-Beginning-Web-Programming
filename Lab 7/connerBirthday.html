It's Conner's Birthday!

Conner's birthday is on November 16th. This year that falls on a Monday. I typically wouldn't do anything for him, but this year everyone seems to want to. I am not sure yet what we are going to do, but I'm almost positive it's gonna be lame.

If you'd like to go, that would be cool, but if you don't want to go, I totally understand that as well. It's probably not going to be that great to be completely honest.

I don't mean to seem like I don't care about Conner's Birthday, but he lives with me, so I see him literally every day all the time. I think for his birthday maybe he should go out of town! That might be ideal for everyone involved!

Overall, I'd like for Conner to have a good party, and I hope all of you will be there, but I probably will not go, as I think his birthday gift from me will be some time away from each other. Plus, I think he wants to do a private screening of Cat's the Musical, and I definitely do NOT want to see that!
